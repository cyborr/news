Manuel de Jesús Rojas Miranda, Another Human Rights violation in Cuba.

Manuel de Jesús Rojas Miranda It’s been six months since my cousin. Manuel de Jesús Rojas Miranda , was kidnapped by State Security. Prior to this event, he had been given a year without detention, for the Crime of Pre-criminal Social Danger (an invention that only exists in Cuba, used by the Dictatorship, to remove those who are annoying ). When there were two months to comply with this arbitrary sanction, they decided to kidnap him.
