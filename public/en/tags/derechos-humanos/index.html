Rick Scott demands from the White House actions in favor of Ferrer and other political prisoners

Senadores Republicanos MIAMI, United States. - Senator Rick Scott (R-F) demanded this Wednesday humanitarian attention for the Cuban opponent José Daniel Ferrer, held for several months in a jail in Santiago de Cuba, and for the hundreds of political prisoners on the island. In telephone conversations with Juan González, senior adviser to the US National Security Council for the Western Hemisphere; António Guterres, Secretary General of the United Nations (UN); and Luis Almagro, secretary general of the Organization of American States (OAS), the legislator emphasized the situation of Ferrer, with whose relatives he also held exchanges.
